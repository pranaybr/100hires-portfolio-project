### 🛠️ What Was Collected
The library maps execution frameworks across four primary tactical pillars:

*   **AI Search Optimization & Infrastructure:** Documenting how generative features pull from foundational SEO frameworks, data-backed visibility triggers, and corporate "Marketing OS" system integrations (*Sara Stella Lattanzio, Diandra Escobar, Tommy Clark*).
*   **Algorithmic Platform Strategy:** Deconstructing the 2026 platform changes—specifically around "360Brew" valuation models, mobile visual pixel constraints, and dwell-time priorities over velocity (*Diandra Escobar, Christopher Lochhead, Anonymous*).
*   **B2B Journey & Attribution Realities:** Analyzing multi-stakeholder enterprise pipelines, long evaluation windows, and company page influence using first-party data (*Brendan Hufford, Devin Reed, Chris Walker*).
*   **Audience Taxonomy & High-Tier Trust:** Breaking down the structural advantages of creator growth and the 8 distinct tiers of social proof that drive actual customer conversions (*Justin Welsh, Katelyn Bourgoin, Neal O'Grady*).
