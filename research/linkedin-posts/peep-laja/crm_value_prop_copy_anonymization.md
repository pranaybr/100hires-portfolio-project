## ── THE RAW SWIPE TEXT ──

We took the value-prop copy from the 5 biggest CRMs and deleted the company names.

Then we asked 100 B2B marketing leaders to match each one back to the brand.

They scored 1.86 out of 5.

No logos, design or layout. Just the words these companies chose duly to describe themselves. Salesforce. HubSpot. Oracle. Zoho. Pipedrive. (Top 5 CRMs selling to the mid-market).

73% of the people guessing were VPs and CMOs of marketing. The rest director-level folks. 

43% got one or zero right. Only 8% got four or five. Random guessing would've scored 1.0.

So they're barely beating a coin flip on copy that real companies paid real money to write.

And they knew it. 74% told us they were "not very confident" or "just guessing."

Your positioning isn't bad because it's wrong. It's bad because it's interchangeable. 

Every CRM says AI-powered, automate your pipeline, close more deals. Delete the name and nobody can tell who's talking.

Buyers aren't confused because they're lazy. They're confused because you sound exactly like the competitor you're trying to beat.

### 2. Core Body Dynamics
