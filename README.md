# B2B SaaS Organic Content Strategy — Research Index

A structured research repository mapping how top B2B SaaS practitioners think about organic distribution, demand generation, and buyer psychology in 2026.

This isn't a listicle. It's a working research base — expert content collected, organized, and annotated for anyone building a content playbook from first principles.

---

## What's in here

- `/research/sources.md` — 10 practitioners with annotations on why they were selected and what angle they cover
- `/research/linkedin-posts/` — posts organized by author
- `/research/youtube-transcripts/` — transcripts organized by video
- `/research/other/` — supplementary materials

---

## How I chose the experts

The selection criteria: people who actually run the plays they teach, not consultants who write about what others should do.

The 10 cover distinct angles without overlapping too much — demand creation vs. lead gen (Chris Walker), zero-click content (Amanda Natividad), B2B brand and community (Dave Gerhardt), messaging and differentiation (Peep Laja), organic social tactics (Sara Stella Lattanzio), LinkedIn content systems (Justin Welsh), SEO-to-social alignment (Brendan Hufford), executive ghostwriting frameworks (Diandra Escobar), buyer psychology and trigger events (Katelyn Bourgoin), and content-to-sales alignment (Devin Reed).

---

## How this was built

I used Cursor and Codex to attempt API-based collection (YouTube transcripts, LinkedIn posts). Git was new to me going into this — the commit history reflects the learning curve.

---

## The throughline

Most of these practitioners are reacting to the same shift: B2B buyers make decisions before they ever fill out a form. Dark social, long lurk periods, community trust — the research here maps how the best operators are building for that reality.
